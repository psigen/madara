Detailed Description

Author:
James Edmondson <james.r.edmondson@vanderbilt.edu>

Macros used for logging in MADARA, based on the DAnCE Log Macros maintained by Will Otte <wotte@dre.vanderbilt.edu>.

Note that though we include MADARA_Logger_export for future expansion possibilities, we are simply including the Log_Macros.h and Log_Macros.cpp files into each project that might need these. Otherwise, KATS users would have to explicitly include Madara.dll or Madara.lib and Kats.dll or Kats.lib instead of just Kats.dll or Kats.lib.
