\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
James Edmondson $<$\href{mailto:james.r.edmondson@vanderbilt.edu}{\tt james.r.edmondson@vanderbilt.edu}$>$
\end{DoxyAuthor}
Macros used for logging in MADARA, based on the DAnCE Log Macros maintained by Will Otte $<$\href{mailto:wotte@dre.vanderbilt.edu}{\tt wotte@dre.vanderbilt.edu}$>$.

Note that though we include MADARA\_\-Logger\_\-export for future expansion possibilities, we are simply including the \hyperlink{Log__Macros_8h}{Log\_\-Macros.h} and \hyperlink{Log__Macros_8cpp}{Log\_\-Macros.cpp} files into each project that might need these. Otherwise, KATS users would have to explicitly include Madara.dll or Madara.lib and Kats.dll or Kats.lib instead of just Kats.dll or Kats.lib.
